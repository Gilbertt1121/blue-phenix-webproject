Mt. Ulap

Mt. Ulap is a popular mountain located in Itogon, Benguet, Philippines. It stands at 1,846 meters above sea level and is well-known for its scenic views, cool climate, and rolling hills. The mountain is often hiked for its stunning panoramas of the surrounding Cordillera mountain range, as well as the picturesque view of the "sea of clouds." It offers a relatively moderate hiking challenge, making it a favorite among outdoor enthusiasts, especially for weekend treks. The trail is surrounded by lush forests and diverse wildlife, adding to the allure of the mountain
Mt. Ulap is part of the Cordillera mountain range in the northern Philippines, specifically situated in the municipality of Itogon, Benguet. It is a favorite trekking destination due to its relatively accessible trails and breathtaking views. The mountain is often associated with the "sea of clouds," where early morning hikers can witness a magical display of fog and mist covering the valleys below.
