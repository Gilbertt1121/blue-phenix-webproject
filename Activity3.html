Burger Medium

a hamburger that is cooked to a medium level of doneness. In terms of preparation, a medium burger is cooked so that the inside is pink and warm but not raw. The patty will have a slight amount of juiciness, with the interior temperature generally around 140°F to 145°F (60°C to 63°C).
